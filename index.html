Strategize,Orangize,Globalize

We are a group of CMU students working alongside the Carnegie Mellon Corporate Startup Lab to research educational tools that will aggregately provide a resource guide for corporate entrepreneurs/innovators.

This website is the final project for 99520 (Experiential Learning through Projects) course with professor Sean Ammirati. We hope this platform can serve as an educational resource guide for corporate entrepreneurs and to make the world a better place using new ideas and innovations.

From our research, we found that Company size is no safeguard when it comes to corporate innovation disruption on the modern business landscape. 88% of Fortune 500 companies in the past few decades have either gone bankrupt, merged with other firms, or dropped off the list for other reasons [Right Mind]. A recent study by McKinsey & Company also found that 80% of executives considered their existing business model to be at risk.
