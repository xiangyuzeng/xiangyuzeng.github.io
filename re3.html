Case Study

The Lean Startup by Eric Ries

Ries is one of the key people leading the lean startup movement. This is a practical guide about what Lean Startup is and how to do it. We love this for being both theoretically accessible and practical.

The Innovator’s Dilemma by Clayton M. Christensen

The author is a Harvard Business Professor who coined the term disruptive innovation. The Innovator’s Dilemma explains how even the most outstanding companies can do everything right yet still lose market leadership.

The Innovator’s Guide to Growth by Scott D. Anthony

In a nod to Christensen’s aforementioned works, these authors show you how to take the practical steps necessary to leverage and benefit from disruption.

The Startup Owner’s Manual by Steve Blank

The Startup Owner's Manual walks you, step-by-step, through the tested and proven Customer Development process, unlocking the secrets to building a successful, scalable company. It's the indispensable reference guide for any startup founder, entrepreneur, investor or educator.
